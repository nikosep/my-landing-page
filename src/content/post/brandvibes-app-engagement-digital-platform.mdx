In today's digital landscape, effective marketing and communication are crucial for businesses to thrive. This is especially true for SMEs, who often lack direct contact with their customers.

## The SaaS Solution: [Brandvibes.app](#)

- Viber-based Mass Marketing Tool

- One-click Customer Onboarding via QR

- Campaign Management Platform

- Analytics Dashboard & Metrics


Brandvibes.app is a SaaS platform tailored for SMEs facing challenges in customer engagement, particularly in physical stores. The solution involves creating a corporate Viber community and a unique QR code. When an anonymous customer scans the QR code while browsing in-store, they instantly become eligible to receive promotions from the business. This seamless solution offers an effortless way for merchants to engage with their customers and drive promotions effectively.
